  • What is Emotional Intelligence?:

    • Emotional intelligence is the ability to percieve, interpret and control emotions to relate to, and communicate with others.

  • How is Emotional Intelligence different from Intelligence Quotient?:

    • IQ looks at your knowledge, your memory and your reasoning in order to solve a problem.

    EQ is your emotional intelligence and looks at your ability to interact with yours, and others, emotions.

  • Why is Emotional Intelligence so important?:

    • Emotional intelligence is essential for working with or in and team. It directly relates with how effectively you can work together. It can help reduce stress within a team, build relationships and improve conflict.
